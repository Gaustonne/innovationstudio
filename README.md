# Pantry Pilot

*Pantry pilot makes managing your kitchen easy*

- Keep track of all your ingredients and their expiries in one place

- Simply tap the plus to add an ingredient, and pantry pilot will alert you when it’s about to expire

- Add a whole shop’s worth at once by snapping a photo of your receipt

- Add individual items easily by scanning their barcodes

- And add fresh produce quickly with intelligent image recognition

- Swipe right on an item to quickly add another three days

- Or swipe left to mark it as wasted

- Track your food wastage with intuitive charts

Staring at a fridge full of ingredients but unsure what to make? Pantry pilot will give you recipes based on what you already have and create shopping lists for what you’re missing

*Take charge of your kitchen, with pantry pilot*
